‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change from Ghoul to CCG or vice versa?
Visit Dr. Kanou in Ward 11 Hospital. It costs 10,000 Yen to switch factions. Note that you'll lose all your current abilities and need to start progression from the beginning in your new faction.
What's the fastest way to get RC Cells?
The fastest way to farm RC Cells is by hunting in Ward 20 Underground. Focus on defeating NPCs with red auras as they drop more RC Cells. Also, complete daily missions which reward 500-1000 RC Cells each.
How do I get a rare Kagune?
Rare Kagunes have a 5% chance when using the Kagune Spin item (costs 2,500 RC Cells). For guaranteed rare Kagunes, collect 10 Kagune Fragments from defeating boss Ghouls and craft them at the Underground Doctor.
When do bosses spawn in the game?
Boss spawns follow this schedule:
- The Owl: Every 3 hours (XX:00, XX:03, etc.)
- Jason: Every 2 hours (XX:30, XX:30, etc.)
- Arima (CCG): Every 4 hours (XX:15, XX:15, etc.)
Boss locations are marked on the map with a red skull icon.
How does the mask system work?
Masks are cosmetic items that also provide stat bonuses. Basic masks give +5% to one stat, while rare masks can give up to +15% to multiple stats. Masks can be obtained from the Mask Shop in Ward 14, from special events, or as drops from boss Ghouls.
