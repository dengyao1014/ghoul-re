Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize controls?

Yes! Open Settings (ESC) → Controls → Key Bindings to customize your controls.

What's the best control setup for PvP?

Many pro players bind abilities to side mouse buttons and use Q/E for dodging.

How do I enable auto-sprint?

Go to Settings → Gameplay → Toggle "Auto Sprint" option.
