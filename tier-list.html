Pro Tips for Kagune Selection

  • Consider your playstyle when choosing a Kagune - mobility-focused players should lean towards Ukaku
  • For beginners, Rinkaku offers the best balance of offense and survivability
  • PvP and farming tier lists can differ significantly - some Kagunes excel in one but struggle in the other
  • Team composition matters - try to complement your squad's existing Kagune types

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my Kagune type?

Visit Dr. Kanou in Ward 11 Hospital with 10,000 RC Cells to reroll your Kagune type. Premium spins guarantee A-tier or higher Kagunes.

What's the best Kagune for solo play?

Rinkaku is generally considered the best for solo play due to its high damage output and self-healing abilities.
