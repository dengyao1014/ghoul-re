How to Create a Clan

Requirements:

  • Reach Level 30 or higher
  • Have 50,000 Yen
  • Complete "Path of Leadership" quest
  • Have at least 5 active friends

Visit the Clan Registrar in Ward 14 to create your clan once you meet all requirements.

Clan Wars Guide

Clan Wars occur every weekend. Here's how to participate and win:

Preparation

  • Ensure at least 10 active members
  • Stock up on RC Cells and healing items
  • Coordinate builds and roles

Territory Control

  • Capture strategic points
  • Defend territory borders
  • Set up patrol routes

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a clan?

You can either receive an invitation from a clan member or apply directly through the clan's profile page.

What happens if I leave a clan?

You'll lose access to clan benefits and must wait 24 hours before joining another clan.

How are clan rankings calculated?

Rankings are based on territory control, war participation, average member level, and weekly clan XP.
