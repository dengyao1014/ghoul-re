GHOUL://RE Controls Guide

Master the game with our complete controls reference for both PC and mobile players.

Basic Controls (PC)

  • W, A, S, D - Movement
  • Space - Jump
  • Shift - Sprint
  • E - Interact
  • Q - Toggle Kagune/Quinque
  • F - Block
  • R - Special Ability
  • 1-5 - Ability Slots
  • M - Menu
  • Tab - Player List

Combat Controls

  • Left Click - Light Attack
  • Right Click - Heavy Attack
  • Shift + Right Click - Special Attack
  • F + Right Click - Counter Attack
  • Space + Direction - Dodge

Mobile Controls

Mobile controls use on-screen buttons for the same functions. The layout can be customized in Settings.

Pro Tip:

Customize your keybinds in the Settings menu for optimal performance. Many top players use custom keybinds for faster ability activation.

  1. Reach Level 30 or higher
  2. Accumulate 50,000 Yen
  3. Visit the Clan Registrar in Ward 14

How to Get Kakuja Fragments in GHOUL://RE

Complete guide to obtaining and using Kakuja Fragments to unlock the powerful Kakuja transformation.

What are Kakuja Fragments?

Kakuja Fragments are rare items that allow Ghouls to evolve their Kagune into a Kakuja form, granting enhanced abilities and a new appearance.

Kakuja Fragment Locations

  • Boss Drops: The Owl (10% chance), Jason (7% chance), Eto (15% chance)
  • Special Events: Kakuja Hunt (occurs every Sunday)
  • Hidden Caches: 5 locations throughout Ward 24 (refreshes weekly)
  • RC Shop: Purchase for 25,000 RC Cells (limit 1 per week)

Kakuja Crafting Process

  1. Collect 10 Kakuja Fragments
  2. Reach Level 75 or higher
  3. Have 100% Ghoul Mastery
  4. Visit Dr. Kanou in the Underground Lab
  5. Pay 50,000 Yen for the procedure
  6. Complete the "Kakuja Transformation" quest

Warning:

Kakuja transformation has a 20% chance of failure if your RC level is below 10,000. Failed transformations result in loss of all fragments and temporary stat penalties.
