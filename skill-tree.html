Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my skill points?

Visit the Skill Master in Ward 11 and pay 5,000 Yen for a skill reset.

When do I get skill points?

You earn 1 skill point every level up, and bonus points from certain achievements.

What's the max level for skills?

Most skills can be leveled up to 5, with each level providing increased benefits.
